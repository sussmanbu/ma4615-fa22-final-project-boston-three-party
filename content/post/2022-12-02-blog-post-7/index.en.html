We are still finalizing our tentative thesis based off our time series, but based of our current data analysis our thesis is as follows. If CO2 emissions continue to increase, paired alongside various other harmful variables related to human development such as population growth, economic growth, and deforestation, then we will see a decrease in the amount of crops produced. Indeed, while deforestation is often seen as a solution for lack of farming productivity, the net effects of climate change seem to outweigh the increase in space for farmland.
